# Inorganic Chemistry · Interactive

**Coordination chemistry, crystal field theory, and organometallics**

**Status:** Planned

Part of [Hengzhe's Science Classroom](https://hengzhe-science-classroom.github.io/hub/) · Tier 4 · Graduate

## What You'll Learn

- Atomic structure and periodicity
- Molecular symmetry and group theory
- Coordination chemistry
- Crystal field and ligand field theory
- Organometallic chemistry
- Bioinorganic chemistry
- Solid-state chemistry

## References

- Gary L. Miessler, Paul J. Fischer & Donald A. Tarr, *Inorganic Chemistry* (5th ed., Pearson)
- Catherine E. Housecroft & Alan G. Sharpe, *Inorganic Chemistry* (5th ed., Pearson)
- J.E. Huheey, E.A. Keiter & R.L. Keiter, *Inorganic Chemistry* (4th ed.)
